About me

I'm a bioinformatics graduate who enjoys turning raw sequencing data into clear, reproducible insight and I build full-stack web apps on the side.

My day-to-day work spans RNA-Seq, whole-genome sequencing, variant calling and multi-omics integration, built in Python and R. For my M.Sc. thesis, I analysed 67 Salmonella enterica genomes end to end — AMR and virulence gene profiling, MLST typing, phylogenetics, and machine-learning feature engineering on top of it.

I also work with machine learning for genomic classification and computational drug discovery (docking, MD, QM/MM), and I build small full-stack web apps for practice. I'm currently finishing my M.Sc. in Bioinformatics at the Central University of Himachal Pradesh, and I'm open to remote roles and relocation.
